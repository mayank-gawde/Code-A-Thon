About Code-a-Thon

🚀 Code-a-Thon 2025 is an exciting 24-hour hackathon that brings together creative minds to innovate and solve real-world problems through technology. 💡💻 Open to all passionate coders, it offers a platform to showcase skills in AI 🤖, Web/App Development 🌐, Cybersecurity 🔐, and more. With three challenging rounds — PPT submission 📑, online hackathon 🖥️, and offline presentation 🎤 — participants will compete for exciting prizes 🏆, internships 💼, and swags 🎁. Join us to Code. Create. Conquer. ⚡ and be part of a dynamic community of developers ready to make an impact! 🌍🔥
